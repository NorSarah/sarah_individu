Mashed Potato Mac and Cheese

Recipe by Jamie Sanders, Adapted from Rachael Ray Everyday Magazine

Prep time: 10
Cook time: 30 to 35 minutes
Total time: 45 minutes

Ingredients

  • 12 oz penne or cavatappi pasta
  • 1 1/4 cup frozen sweet corn, defrosted
  • Olive oil
  • 1/4 cup minced meat freshly cooked and diced
  • 2 cups peeled, diced potato
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped into 1/4-inch cubes
  • 1 red onion, finely chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • Salt and black pepper
  • 3 tbs butter
  • 3 tbs flour
  • 2 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan blend cheese
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded sharp yellow cheddar cheese

Cooking Instructions

  1. Cook pasta in large pot, according to package directions. After cooking is complete, drain, return to pot and keep warm.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, add oil to pan and brown potatoes for about 5 minutes.
  3. Add corn, red pepper, onion and garlic, salt and pepper to the potatoes. Cook for another 5 to 10 minutes.
  4. Meanwhile, melt the butter in a medium size saucepan over medium heat. Whisk in the flour. Add milk and whisk til smooth and add salt with pepper. Cook until the sauce thickens.
  5. Lower the heat and stir in the Parmigiano Reggiano.
  6. Add the corn-potato mixture and the white sauce to the pasta in the pot and stir together.
  7. Transfer to a casserole dish, top with the cheddar cheese, minced meat and reserved red bell pepper. Broil until brown and bubbly, about 5 minutes.
